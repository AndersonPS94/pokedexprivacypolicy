Privacy Policy

Pokédex Application

Introduction

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Pokédex (the “App”), developed by AndersonPS (the “Developer”), handles information from its users (each, a “User”).

The Developer is fully committed to protecting User privacy. This policy is designed to be clear, objective, and transparent, ensuring full compliance with the Google Play User Data Policy.

1. Collection of Personal Data

The Pokédex App DOES NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or personal data from its Users.

The App is designed to operate without collecting, transmitting, or storing any information that could identify an individual, such as name, email address, precise location, persistent device identifiers, or any other sensitive information.

2. Use of Data

The Pokédex App is solely intended to provide Pokémon information. To achieve this, the App performs the following operations:

  • Public API Consumption: The App uses a public API to retrieve and display Pokémon information. This data is public and not associated with any User.
  • Local Cache: The App stores Pokémon information locally on the User's device using a Room Database for caching and offline functionality. This cached data is operational only and not transmitted to the Developer or any third parties.

No User data is used for advertising, analytics, tracking, or any commercial purpose.

3. Sharing of Data with Third Parties

The Pokédex App DOES NOT share ANY User data with third parties.

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no data to share. The App does not use third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics, advertising SDKs, or any tracking technologies.

4. Data Retention

The local Pokémon information cached on the User’s device is retained only while the App remains installed. The Developer has no access to these operational data and does not retain them.

5. Data Security

Although the App does not collect personal data, the Developer takes reasonable measures to ensure the security of operational data (Pokémon cache) stored locally. The App does not request sensitive Android permissions, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Pokédex App is NOT intended for children under 13 years old. However, because the App does not collect personal data, the privacy of all Users — regardless of age — is protected by design.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When changes are made, the “Last Updated” date at the top will be adjusted. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App’s practices, please contact the Developer:

  • Developer Name: AndersonPS
  • Email: andersonpsdev@gmail.com

Google Play Compliance Statement

The Pokédex App fully complies with the Google Play User Data Policy, as it does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive User data. The App operates with minimal permissions and focuses exclusively on delivering public content from an external API.
